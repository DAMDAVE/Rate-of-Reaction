Introduction to Chemical Kinetics

Chemical Kinetics is the field of chemistry that deals with the study of the rates of reactions. It provides information on;

  • How long it takes a reaction to occur
  • How fast a reaction goes
  • How the rate can be measured and;
  • The pathways through which the reactants are converted into products
