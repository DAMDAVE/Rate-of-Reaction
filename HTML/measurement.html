Different parameters for measuring Rate of chemical reactions

    Many parameters can be used to measure Rate of Reaction, there are a couple number of them;

  1. Concentration
  2. Mass
  3. Volume
  4. Precipitate Formed
  5. PH Changes
  6. Colour intensity
  7. Total Gas Pressure
  8. Volume of Gaseous product
  9. E . T . C

Any of these parameters can form a basis for the calculation of the rate of reaction, simply put; Change in any of these params with respect to time is a totally legal definition of rate of reaction
Now, did we not say otherwise in the previous page.

But what do I mean by what is written in italic? Well, let's take concentration as an example
We can say; Rate is the change of concentration of rectant and product with respect to time
or if it is mass, we can say Rate is the change of mass of reactant and product with respect to time.
